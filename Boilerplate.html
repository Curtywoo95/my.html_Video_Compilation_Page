Bread Sandwich

Today I am going to teach you how to make your own bread sandwich at home!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • More bread

Instructions

  1. Take firt slice of bread and butter one side.
  2. Take second slice of bread and butter one side.
  3. Take third slice of bread and butter both sides.
  4. Place third slice of bread on the buttered side of slice one.
  5. Place second slice buttered side down ontop of third slice.
  6. Enjoy!
